Datum orientation: Use the cylinder axis as X. The left faced end is datum A at X=0. The radial direction through the largest flat or slot is datum B. Positive Z points upward from the part centerline when the main flat faces up.

Full description:

Make the single metal part for the product idea 'Adjustable Olympic Barbell Safety Catch Hook'. The broader use case is: Power rack attachment for catching a loaded barbell during squats or bench press The chosen deliverable is only the metal body implied by: Machined steel hook body with rack pin bore, radiused bar cradle, rear locating boss, and gusseted load path All other product elements are external reference items and must not be modeled. Tolerances: - Outside diameter and axial bore diameter: +/-0.05 mm. - Concentricity of bore to outer diameter: within 0.05 mm TIR. - Milled flats and slots: +/-0.15 mm unless otherwise specified. - Nonfunctional chamfers: +/-0.3 mm.
