Agent 023 Single Metal Part Requirements

Domain: warehouse logistics, carts, conveyor accessories, pallet handling, tote systems, and material-flow hardware

Each item below is one metal part only. Product assemblies and nonmetal components are explicitly out of scope.

SMP-023-01 - Adjustable Conveyor Side Guide Bracket

Part name: Adjustable Conveyor Side Guide Bracket - single metal part

Material: low-carbon steel, ASTM A36 or equivalent (general-purpose single-piece metal stock)

Envelope: {"length_mm": 220, "thickness_mm": 6, "width_mm": 65}

Datum orientation: Use the finished bottom face as datum A. Use the long left edge as datum B and the near short edge as datum C. The origin is the lower-left-near corner of the finished rectangular envelope; X follows length, Y follows width, and Z is upward.

Full description:

Make the single metal part for the product idea 'Adjustable Conveyor Side Guide Bracket'. The broader use case is: Used along roller or belt conveyors in warehouses to keep cartons, totes, and trays aligned during transport. The chosen deliverable is only the metal body implied by: Machined L-shaped bracket with elongated slots, clamp face, gusset ribs, and rail-mount holes. All other product elements are external reference items and must not be modeled. The part must perform this mechanical role: Provides a rigid mounting point for a side guide rail with slotted adjustment for product width changes. It is intentionally included in the SubCAD limit corpus because: Tests slots, orthogonal mounting faces, rib-like reinforcements, clearance offsets, and repeated hole patterns in a functional industrial bracket. The part is made from low-carbon steel, ASTM A36 or equivalent using sheet or plate stock. Start from one flat sheet or plate blank. Cut the outside profile, machine holes, slots, pockets, lips, and relief features into that same piece. If bends are called out, they are bends in the same sheet part, not separate welded pieces.

Functional features: - single rectangular metal body: Machine one body with finished envelope 220 mm x 65 mm x 6 mm. - mounting hole pattern: Drill two through holes diameter 6 mm on the length centerline at X=13 mm and X=207 mm, Y=32 mm. - functional center feature: Machine a central obround slot 73 mm long x 8 mm wide through the part, centered at X=110 mm, Y=32 mm. - top relief pocket: Mill a rectangular relief pocket 55 mm x 21 mm x 2 mm deep on the top face, centered between the mounting holes. - edge chamfers: Apply 1.0 mm x 45 degree chamfers to all top outside edges and 0.5 mm chamfers to hole and slot mouths.

Tolerances: - Overall envelope dimensions: +/-0.20 mm. - Hole diameters and slot widths: +/-0.10 mm. - Hole and slot center positions from datums B and C: +/-0.15 mm. - Pocket depths and relief depths: +/-0.10 mm. - Nonfunctional chamfers and radii: +/-0.30 mm.
