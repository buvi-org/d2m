SMP-024-01 - Rail Clip Tension Verification Gauge

Part name: Rail Clip Tension Verification Gauge - single metal part

Material: low-carbon steel, ASTM A36 or equivalent (general-purpose single-piece metal stock)

Envelope: {"length_mm": 230, "thickness_mm": 5, "width_mm": 135}

Datum orientation: Use the finished bottom face as datum A. Use the long left edge as datum B and the near short edge as datum C. The origin is the lower-left-near corner of the finished rectangular envelope; X follows length, Y follows width, and Z is upward.

Full description:

Make the single metal part for the product idea 'Rail Clip Tension Verification Gauge'. The broader use case is: Railway track maintenance crews checking elastic rail fastening clips during inspection rounds. The chosen deliverable is only the metal body implied by: Stepped bridge gauge body with rail-foot locating pads, clip clearance slot, and probe reference boss. All other product elements are external reference items and must not be modeled. The part must perform this mechanical role: Slides over a rail clip and provides a mechanical reference surface for measuring whether clip deflection remains within service limits. It is intentionally included in the SubCAD limit corpus because: Requires nested cutouts, asymmetric stepped surfaces, datum pads, rail-profile clearances, and functional gauge features that must relate precisely to one another. The part is made from low- carbon steel, ASTM A36 or equivalent using sheet or plate stock. Start from one flat sheet or plate blank. Cut the outside profile, machine holes, slots, pockets, lips, and relief features into that same piece. If bends are called out, they are bends in the same sheet part, not separate welded pieces.

Functional features: - single rectangular metal body: Machine one body with finished envelope 230 mm x 135 mm x 5 mm. - mounting hole pattern: Drill two through holes diameter 11 mm on the length centerline at X=27 mm and X=203 mm, Y=67 mm. - functional center feature: Machine a central obround slot 76 mm long x 14 mm wide through the part, centered at X=115 mm, Y=67 mm. - top relief pocket: Mill a rectangular relief pocket 57 mm x 45 mm x 1 mm deep on the top face, centered between the mounting holes. - edge chamfers: Apply 1.0 mm x 45 degree chamfers to all top outside edges and 0.5 mm chamfers to hole and slot mouths. - serrated contact edge: Cut 9 equal triangular serrations across the rear edge, each 5 mm deep. - integral hook lip: Leave an integral hook lip on one short end, projecting 16 mm and undercut 5 mm for registration.

Tolerances: - Overall envelope dimensions: +/-0.20 mm. - Hole diameters and slot widths: +/-0.10 mm. - Hole and slot center positions from datums B and C: +/-0.15 mm. - Pocket depths and relief depths: +/-0.10 mm. - Nonfunctional chamfers and radii: +/-0.30 mm.
