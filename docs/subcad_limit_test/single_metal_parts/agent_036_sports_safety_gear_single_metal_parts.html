Agent 036 Single Metal Part Requirements

Domain: wearable sports safety gear, helmet mounts, protective equipment hardware, and adjustment mechanisms

Each item below is one metal part only. Product assemblies and nonmetal components are explicitly out of scope.

SMP-036-01 - Rotating helmet visor quick-lock hinge

Part name: Rotating helmet visor quick-lock hinge - single metal part

Material: low-carbon steel, ASTM A36 or equivalent (general-purpose single-piece metal stock)

Envelope: {"length_mm": 180, "thickness_mm": 4, "width_mm": 145}

Datum orientation: Use the finished bottom face as datum A. Use the long left edge as datum B and the near short edge as datum C. The origin is the lower-left-near corner of the finished rectangular envelope; X follows length, Y follows width, and Z is upward.

Full description:

Make the single metal part for the product idea 'Rotating helmet visor quick-lock hinge'. The broader use case is: Cycling and skating helmets that need removable or adjustable face shields. The chosen deliverable is only the metal body implied by: Detented hinge bracket with integrated latch tabs. All other product elements are external reference items and must not be modeled. The part must perform this mechanical role: Allows a visor to pivot between raised and lowered positions while locking securely under vibration. It is intentionally included in the SubCAD limit corpus because: Combines rotational clearance, snap-fit retention, asymmetric mounting ears, and repeated small locking features around a curved helmet interface. The part is made from low-carbon steel, ASTM A36 or equivalent using sheet or plate stock. Start from one flat sheet or plate blank. Cut the outside profile, machine holes, slots, pockets, lips, and relief features into that same piece. If bends are called out, they are bends in the same sheet part, not separate welded pieces.

Functional features: - single rectangular metal body: Machine one body with finished envelope 180 mm x 145 mm x 4 mm. - mounting hole pattern: Drill two through holes diameter 5 mm on the length centerline at X=29 mm and X=151 mm, Y=72 mm. - functional center feature: Machine a central obround slot 60 mm long x 13 mm wide through the part, centered at X=90 mm, Y=72 mm. - top relief pocket: Mill a rectangular relief pocket 45 mm x 48 mm x 1 mm deep on the top face, centered between the mounting holes. - edge chamfers: Apply 1.0 mm x 45 degree chamfers to all top outside edges and 0.5 mm chamfers to hole and slot mouths.

Tolerances: - Overall envelope dimensions: +/-0.20 mm. - Hole diameters and slot widths: +/-0.10 mm. - Hole and slot center positions from datums B and C: +/-0.15 mm. - Pocket depths and relief depths: +/-0.10 mm. - Nonfunctional chamfers and radii: +/-0.30 mm.
